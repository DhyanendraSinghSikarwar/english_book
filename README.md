# Corporate English Mastery
### From Intermediate to Advanced Business English for the Global Workplace
**For Indian professionals moving to European (English-speaking) corporate environments — with a Data Science career focus**

This book is written in **simple English** so it's easy to read, even though it teaches advanced professional English. Every part is packed with ready-to-use sentences, real workplace scenarios, and notes on Indian-English habits to adjust.

**Entry format used throughout:** Definition → Examples → Real Workplace Scenario → When/How to Use → Indian-English Pitfall to Avoid.

---

## How to read this book

Each chapter is a separate markdown file inside the `english_book` folder. Read them in order for a full course, or jump to the part you need. New words and skills build on earlier ones. Use Part 10 (practice) and the Appendices as you go.

## A quick note on the philosophy of this book

Two ideas run through every chapter:

1. **Clarity over showing off.** Professional English isn't about big words. It's about being clear, calm, and understood. Upgrade your language for formal moments, but stay simple and warm the rest of the time.

2. **Directness is respect.** The biggest cultural shift from India to Europe is directness — honest opinions, clear "no"s, first names, and results over hierarchy. It feels bold at first, but it's exactly what European colleagues expect and respect.

Learn a little each week, practise out loud, use the phrases in real work, and come back often. You already have the skills — this book gives you the words.

Good luck with your move. You've got this.
